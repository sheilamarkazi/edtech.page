Robotics in the Classroom

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

I created this website to document a typical experience in my engineering classes and provides insight into my educational values.

I lead students by enabling their creative freedom as they tackle new and challenging technical projects. My process begins with placing students at an intersection of engineering, design, music, and art. Then, I help them acquire technical skills and manage their progress as they create.
