Laser Cutter Tutorial

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

This eLearning course is designed to teach high school engineering students how to operate an advanced laser system for cutting and engraving. In the process, students learn the basics of Adobe Illustrator and create their own designs. This course leads students through video tutorials, an interactive simulation, a reference guide, and form-based assessment questions. Ultimately, learners operate the laser system to print their unique design under safe supervision from the instructor.

The final report includes a needs assessment, design considerations, and evaluation methods. The documentation includes an implementation guide and assessment materials to help an instructor easily integrate this instructional unit into their classroom.


TOOLS USED

Articulate Storyline, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Premiere Pro, Amazon Web Services Simple Storage Service (AWS S3), and Google Workspace.
